Kenny's Wedding Speech(English)

Jiseong, my love. You always told me you wanted to be someone cool in front of me.

But to me, you already are the coolest person in the world. During our countless video calls, you would quietly paint with so much focus and grace.

You are like a painting yourself — someone who brings me peace just by being there.

When you run to catch the bus to work, I always watch you from behind, and somehow it makes me think I should try harder in life too.

The first time I got sick in Korea, you spent two hours going back and forth just to leave food and medicine at my door.

You were so cool then too. I ate the food you brought me, and at the same time, I learned what love really is.

Sometimes I wake up in the middle of the night and cannot fall back asleep. Even while still asleep, you reach your arm over to hold me.

Whenever that happens, I feel like a lost child who has finally found home. And every time you come home from work and run toward me, telling me how much you missed me — that version of you is so cool too.

Your words, your bright smile, the way you chase after me around the house… they always fill the empty places inside my heart.

The way you cook so many amazing meals at home is so cool too. Sometimes I honestly wonder what I ever did to deserve you.

But the coolest thing of all is that you make me smile every time you cross my mind.

How do you do it, I wonder? It is okay if you do not want to tell me. Magicians never reveal their secrets.

Can you just promise me that you will keep using this magic on me for the next thousand years?

And I promise you this: as long as I can still lift these arms, they will always be here to hold you and protect you.

The coolest person in the world. My sweetest Honey. My cutest Adora. And the home I had been searching for my whole life.

I love you.
