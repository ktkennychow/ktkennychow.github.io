Jiseong's Wedding Speech(English)

Kenny is the warmest, kindest, and brightest person I have ever met in my life. I believe he’s someone who makes this world a more beautiful place by always caring for the people around him, even those he doesn’t know.

Kenny never turns away from people who need help. On the subway, he always looks out for people who may need a seat more than he does and gladly gives up his own.

When he sees a woman struggling to carry a pram up the stairs alone, he does not hesitate to lift it for her, and he always holds the door open for the people walking behind him.

Those small moments helped me realise how much warmth and kindness Kenny carries within him, and showed me the many wonderful sides of who he is.

Through meeting Kenny, I feel that my perspective on the world has broadened, and that I’ve truly learned so many things.

To be honest, I do not think I used to see “differences” in a particularly positive way.

However, after meeting Kenny, I came to realise that when we move beyond those walls and learn to understand one another, those differences can open the door to a far more beautiful world and a much broader perspective.

While preparing for the wedding, I met many friends and was asked this question countless times: “Why do you want to marry this person?” I think this is the perfect moment to share my answer with everyone.

I feel that I have become a better person than I was before meeting Kenny. And I truly like the person I have become.

The person you choose to spend your life with can shape your world in completely different ways, almost like colours blending together on a canvas.

Kenny is the person with the warmest heart and most beautiful colours I have ever seen, and with those colours, he always brings out the best and most beautiful parts of me.
